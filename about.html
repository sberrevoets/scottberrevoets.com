About

Hi, I'm Scott! Back in 2014 I moved to San Francisco as one of Lyft's first iOS hires, and I still work there now. I've seen and helped the apps grow from a monolithic Objective-C codebase in 2014 to a highly-sophisticated class of apps and underlying technologies today.

I'm currently on Lyft's Mobile Infra team, where I look after the quality and scalability of our iOS and Android codebases. In the past I was also a tech editor and video instructor at raywenderlich.com.

I was also involved in:

  • Lyft Mobile Podcast part 1 & part 2
  • Advanced Auto Layout at RWDevCon 2017
  • Keeping the Lyft App Accessible

Feel free to reach out if you want to hear more!
