<div class="card-grid focus-grid">
<div class="card focus-card architecture-card">
<h3>Architecture and migrations</h3>
<p>I help large codebases evolve without freezing product work. At Lyft, that
meant modularizing the iOS apps, introducing <a href="{filename}../blog/2021-10-14-lyft-ios-architecture.md">state-driven architecture patterns</a>,
and designing migrations that dozens of engineers could adopt incrementally.</p>
</div>

<div class="card focus-card devex-card">
<h3>Developer experience</h3>
<p>I build tools and feedback loops that make engineering work easier to trust,
from code quality dashboards, ownership systems, and <a href="{filename}../blog/2024-11-19-testing-infrastructure.md">testing strategy</a> at Lyft to mobile
observability and simplified release workflows at Speak.</p>
</div>

<div class="card focus-card platform-card">
<h3>Platform engineering</h3>
<p>I create shared systems that product teams can build on instead of
rebuilding. At Speak, that includes CI, release automation, authentication
infrastructure, and other foundations for a fast-moving mobile product.</p>
</div>
